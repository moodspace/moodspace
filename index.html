Alex aka MoodSpace

Suzhou / Hong Kong / Ithaca

I created my first program on a DIY computer running the first gen Pentium 4 processor. The 'program' was really some excel worksheet decorated by UI controls and VBA macros. Microsoft still has those controls in its office suite, so just imagine how many others around there who learned programming while tinkering tools that won't be used by most coders today.

Before Cornell, I was a blog writer. The blog has moved to a host of web hosts... due to the Internet lockdown in China . Initially named Moonday, the blog has changed its name to MoodSpace, thus my codename.

Now I am a junior undergrad majoring in Computer Science and in Information Science. I started working on the Student Assembly's Technology Committee, leading a subgroup that develops a better course management platform. I have strong interest in full stack development and data-driven applications. A great fan of Linux OSs, I use FOSS.
